# ISO42001 & AI Management Systems

## What is ISO 42001?

> “ISO/IEC 42001 is an international standard that specifies requirements for establishing,
implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Artificial Intelligence Management System
(AIMS) within organizations. It is designed for entities providing or utilizing AI-based products or
services, ensuring responsible development and use of AI systems. ” – ISO

Any orgs that use, develop or work with AI products. The management system that works you move everything under one umbrella.

## Why ISO 42001?

Establishes a system that allows you to ensure you are comfortable with your AI standards. Not prescriptive, but lets you ensure that you are complying with best practices etc.

Things such as how the org handles data, how the PII is regulated. Also what the auditor is looking at. Only the single product can be certified, not the entire organisation.

## PDCA Cycle

Plan, Do, Check, Act cycle.

Plan includes:

- Understand the organisation
- identify stakeholders 

Do includes:

- Implement standards and plan

Check includes:

- Monitor AI systems
- Active involvement in ensuring meeting standards and governance

Act includes:

- Identify areas of concern in system, eg function and performance
- Identify standards and plans that might be improved
